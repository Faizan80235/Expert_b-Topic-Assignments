What is pure milk?
Pure milk means the whole of the milk (including what is commonly known as the strippings) drawn at the time of
milking; but does not include milk containing less than 3 percent of butterfat, or mixed with any preservative
or chemical or colouring matter of any kind: Sample 1.a
